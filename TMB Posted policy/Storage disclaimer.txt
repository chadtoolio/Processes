Vehicles must be picked up and the accompanying invoice must be paid in full within five (5) days of the completion of repairs to the vehicle.
 On the sixth (6th) day after completion of repairs to the vehicle, a storage fee of $100.00 will be added to the vehicle's invoice. 
Beginning on the seventh (7th) day after completion of repairs to the vehicle and for each day thereafter until the vehicle is picked up and the accompanying 
invoice is paid in full, an additional storage fee of $20.00 per day will be added to the vehicle's invoice. 
A vehicle that is not picked up within thirty (30) days with the accompanying invoice paid in full is subject to public auction pursuant to South Carolina law.
